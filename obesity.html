Let's Fight Obesity!

What is Obesity?

"Obesity is health condition characterized by excessive accumalation of body fat."

Over 70 million adults in the United States are obese. This number is surely very large, but there are many possible solutions to avoid and reduce obesity.

People who have obesity are at an increased risk for many serious diseases and health conditions like; Stroke,High blood pressure,Type 2 diabetes, Breathing problems, and etc. The sooner you start, the faster your life quality improves!

These Are All The Solutions For The Obesity Epidemic That We Can All Contribute and Help:

  • Eating fruits; grapefruit, kiwi, melons,apples, berries are all low calorie fruits great for weight loss
  • Cut down consumption of fatty and sugary food; instead drink fruit juices, yogurt, etc
  • Start following a diet such as keto diet
  • Exercise for atleast 30 minutes a day; walking, jogging, swimming, etc.
